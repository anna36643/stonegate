About

Stonegate Theatre
ul. Grodzka 28
31-044 Kraków

A theatre built on the conviction that old texts ask new questions.

Stonegate was founded in 1891 in a former granary on Grodzka Street. The building still has its original stone walls. The programming has changed considerably.

We do not produce heritage. We produce theatre — work that takes a text written centuries ago and asks why it has not yet gone quiet. Every production begins with the same question: what does this play know that we have forgotten?

Our ensemble is permanent. Our designers work across seasons, not projects. The result is a house with a recognisable aesthetic: spare, stone-dark, attentive to language.
